Pharma Industry

Overview

The pharma industry demands high precision, hygiene, and continuous operations. Even minor failures can impact compliance, production quality, and delivery timelines.

Common Observations

  • Frequent bearing failures due to contamination
  • Improper lubrication practices
  • Unplanned machine downtime

Downtime Impact

Downtime leads to batch losses, compliance risks, delayed production, and significant financial impact.

Where Things Go Wrong

  • Incorrect product selection
  • Lack of preventive maintenance
  • Improper handling and storage
