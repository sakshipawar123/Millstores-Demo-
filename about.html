About Millstores

Building trust through reliability and excellence since 1995

Our Founder's Vision

"I value relationships over business"

– R.K. Mantri, Founder & CEO

In 1995, R.K. Mantri founded Millstores with a simple yet powerful vision: to be India's most trusted industrial reliability partner. What started as a small operation has grown into one of India's leading suppliers of industrial maintenance and spare parts.

With over 28 years of dedicated service, Millstores has become the preferred choice for businesses across diverse industries including pharmaceuticals, steel, chemicals, ports, food & beverage, and many others. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us synonymous with industrial excellence.

Today, Millstores operates with a team of passionate professionals who understand the critical nature of industrial operations. We don't just sell products; we build lasting relationships based on trust, transparency, and exceptional service. Our mission is to reduce downtime, improve operational efficiency, and help your business thrive.

We pride ourselves on being more than a supplier—we are a trusted partner in your journey toward operational excellence and sustainable business growth.

R.K. Mantri

Founder & CEO
