Steel Industry

Overview

Steel plants operate in extreme conditions involving heat, dust, and heavy loads. Reliability and durability of components are critical.

Common Observations

  • Bearing failures due to high temperature
  • Heavy load wear and tear
  • Lubrication breakdown

Downtime Impact

Downtime leads to production loss, energy wastage, and delayed deliveries.

Where Things Go Wrong

  • Incorrect bearing selection
  • Poor lubrication systems
  • Lack of predictive maintenance
